What is DateMaker?

DateMaker is an itinerary planning app that helps you discover local venues, restaurants, and events to plan the perfect outing. Whether it's a date night, friend hangout, or solo adventure - we help you plan it!

How do I create an itinerary?

Simply enter your location, select your interests and preferred activities, set your start time and duration, then tap "Generate Date" to get a personalized itinerary with venue recommendations.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription by visiting thedatemakerapp.com and logging into your account. From there, you can update your payment method, view billing history, or cancel your subscription.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to Profile ‚Üí scroll down to "Danger Zone" ‚Üí tap "Delete My Account". This will permanently remove all your data. You can also email us to request account deletion.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure authentication through Firebase. We never share your personal information with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Close and reopen the app, 2) Check your internet connection, 3) Make sure you have the latest version. If problems persist, email us with details about the issue.

We love hearing from our users! Send your bug reports or feature suggestions to thedatemakerapp@outlook.com and we'll review them for future updates.
